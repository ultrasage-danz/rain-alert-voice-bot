# Rain Alert Voice Bot

A background Telegram bot that watches the minute-by-minute rain forecast and,
when rain is about to start, sends you a spoken voice note telling you what to
do — grab an umbrella, close your windows, get inside, or run — depending on
whether you're currently indoors or out.

Built for DEV's Weekend Challenge: Passion Edition (ElevenLabs prize category).

## Why this exists
Built out of genuine frustration with daily rain and constantly
having to check the sky or a weather app. This runs on its own and pings you
with a voice note instead.
